Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Talk with God (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

The Talk with God app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

User-generated content (such as text entered into the app) is processed solely to provide the core functionality of the app and is not sold or shared with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

Use of Data

Any data processed by the app is used exclusively to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Support subscription access where applicable

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or behavioral tracking.

Subscriptions

Talk with God offers an optional auto-renewable subscription (“Talk with God Plus”). Subscription purchases and management are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The app may rely on Apple-provided services (such as StoreKit) and platform infrastructure to operate. These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are used to protect the integrity and security of the app. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe such information has been provided inadvertently, please contact us so it can be removed.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
